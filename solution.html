Aerospace & Space Fasteners

Precision Fasteners for Flight and Beyond

Precision Fasteners for Flight and Beyond. At Nitha Aerospace, we design and manufacture aerospace and space-grade fasteners built for uncompromising performance. From commercial aircraft to orbital systems, our components are trusted in high-stakes environments where precision, strength, and reliability are non-negotiable.

Aerospace Applications

  • Aircraft Structures (airframes, wings, landing gear)
  • Jet Engines and Turbines
  • Avionics and Control Systems
  • Helicopters and Rotor Assemblies

Space Applications

  • Satellites and Payload Structures
  • Launch Vehicles and Boosters
  • Deep Space Probes
  • Space Station Modules

Surface Finishes

  • Cadmium Plating (Silver, Gold/Yellow, Black)
  • Moly Coating – Dry film lubricant

Premium Materials

  • Titanium Grade 5 – Lightweight, corrosion-resistant
  • Inconel 718 – High-temperature nickel alloy
  • A286 & 15-5PH Stainless Steel
  • 30NCD16, 35NCD16, E40CDV20 – Heat-treated alloy steels

Thread Types

  • UNJF – Aerospace inch thread
  • MJ – Metric aerospace profile
  • Metric Fine – Tight tolerance threads

Size Range

  • Metric: M3 to M64
  • Inch: 1/8" to 2-9/16"

Why Choose Us?

  • Precision-machined
  • Fully traceable
