WHAT WE DO

Global One-Stop Solution for Engineering Materials

From melting to plating, we manage the science behind every layer of metal. Our facilities unite metallurgy, machining, heat treatment, and coating under one roof. Delivering global-grade engineering materials and performance assurance.

CASTING

Our fastener manufacturing process begins with high-integrity casting for ferrous, non-ferrous, and superalloy fasteners. This ensures consistent strength and durability, essential for aerospace, space & aviation, and oil & gas applications.

FORGING

Our forging solutions for fasteners that require superior grain flow and structural strength. Our forged fasteners are ideal for high-stress aerospace environments and critical industrial uses where performance is non-negotiable.

PRECISION MACHINING

Equipped with advanced CNC machining technology, we produce precision fasteners with tight tolerances and complex geometries. Our machining supports aerospace-grade threads such as MJ and UNJF, meeting exacting aerospace specifications.

HEAT TREATMENT

Our in-house heat treatment facility enhances the mechanical properties of our fasteners. Using controlled thermal cycles, we improve hardness, fatigue strength, and durability — critical for oil & gas, energy, and aerospace fastener performance.

SURFACE TREATMENT

We provide advanced surface treatment solutions including plating, passivation, and anti-corrosion coatings. These treatments extend fastener life in extreme conditions, ensuring compliance with aerospace, marine, and industrial fastener standards.

R&D TESTING

Our dedicated R&D and testing laboratory ensures every fastener meets the highest quality standards. From tensile strength to metallurgical analysis, we validate each product for aerospace and critical industrial applications.
