About NAPL

NITHA Aerospace Private Limited is an engineering-driven manufacturer of aerospace-grade fasteners and precision components, supported by Indo-European applied industrial R&D capabilities.

Powered by FASTENERS INTELLIGENCE SYSTEM™ (FIS™) we deliver traceable, test-validated fasteners for Aerospace, Defence, Oil & Gas and other critical industries.

NAPL is a precision engineering company delivering high-performance fasteners and components to the Automotive, Aerospace, Defence, Oil & Gas, and Motorsport industries worldwide. Our products are renowned for their strength, reliability, and endurance performing flawlessly under dynamic loads, vibration, and extreme temperatures.

Our Indo-European R&D laboratories specialize in applied industrial research and the technological advancement of key metal sectors, including aluminium, copper, alloy steel, brass, composites, titanium, and zinc.

We offer cutting-edge R&D services and technical solutions for new product development, while also optimizing existing products, business operations, and plant production processes. With deep expertise in precision threads, tensile strength (800–1200 MPa), and custom tolerances, we ensure every product meets rigorous international quality standards. Our advanced surface treatments—Zinc/Nickel Plating, Moly Coating, and Phosphate Passivation enhance corrosion resistance and durability under demanding conditions.

NAPL empowers OEMs and Tier-1 suppliers across the globe to build stronger, safer, and more efficient systems through engineering excellence.

Our facilities unite Metallurgy, Machining, Heat Treatment, Thread Rolling and Coating under one roof. Delivering Global-Grade Engineering Materials and performance assurance.

“from Melting to Plating, we engineer the science behind every layer of metal.”
