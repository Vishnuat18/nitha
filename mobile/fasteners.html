What We Do

Precision Fasteners Engineered to Perform

At NAPL, we don't just manufacture fasteners — we engineer confidence in every connection. Specializing in super alloy fasteners for aerospace, oil & gas, energy, and space systems.

Our solutions are built for environments where failure isn't an option — from high-vibration jet engines to thermal extremes in space.

Precision Driven

Industries 4.0-enabled facility integrating smart processes and real-time quality monitoring.

Materials Matter

Working with Titanium, Inconel, A286, and Monel superalloys for unmatched strength.

End-to-End

Controlling the entire lifecycle in-house to guarantee performance and traceability.
