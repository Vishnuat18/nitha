Fasteners Intelligence System™

Engineering Intelligence

FIS is NAPL’s proprietary engineering framework governing the design, validation, manufacture, inspection, and traceability of safety-critical joints.

Talk to an FIS Engineer →

Why FIS Exists

Hidden Assumptions

Preload, friction, and fatigue behavior are often assumed — not validated — during fastener selection.

Compliance ≠ Validation

Meeting a manufacturing standard does not guarantee joint integrity under real service conditions.

Systemic Failure

Most failures occur due to system-level gaps — not just material defects alone.

With FIS, design becomes compliance. Without validation, design is intent.

FIS Lifecycle

The Interactive Framework

FIS™ Lifecycle
01

Design

Application-driven joint design including material selection, thread geometry, and service conditions.

02

Validate

Engineering validation using VDI 2230 and NASA-STD-5020 standards for safety verification.

03

Manufacture

Process-controlled production covering melting, forming, machining, and surface finishing.

04

Inspect

Dimensional and metallurgical inspection aligned with customer and regulatory requirements.

05

Trace

End-to-end batch and process traceability documentation retained for the full program lifecycle.
