About NAPL

NITHA Aerospace Private Limited (NAPL) is an engineering-driven manufacturer of aerospace-grade fasteners and precision components, supported by Indo-European applied industrial R&D capabilities.

Powered by FASTENERS INTELLIGENCE SYSTEM™ (FIS™), we deliver traceable, test-validated fasteners for Aerospace, Defence, Oil & Gas and other critical industries.

NAPL is a precision engineering company delivering high-performance fasteners and components to the Automotive, Aerospace, Defence, Oil & Gas, and Motorsport industries worldwide.

Our products are renowned for their strength, reliability, and endurance, performing flawlessly under dynamic loads, vibration, and extreme temperatures.

Our Indo-European R&D laboratories specialize in applied industrial research and technological advancement across aluminium, copper, alloy steel, brass, composites, titanium, and zinc.

We provide cutting-edge R&D services for new product development while optimizing existing products, manufacturing operations, and plant production processes.

With expertise in precision threads, tensile strength (800–1200 MPa), and custom tolerances, every product meets stringent international quality standards.

Advanced surface treatments such as Zinc/Nickel Plating, Moly Coating, and Phosphate Passivation enhance corrosion resistance and durability under demanding conditions.

Our integrated facilities bring together Metallurgy, Machining, Heat Treatment, Thread Rolling, and Coating under one roof, delivering global-grade engineering materials with assured performance.

Core Values

Our values guide our decisions

At NAPl, we don't just manufacture fasteners - we build trust, ensure reliability, and uphold standards that define critical industries. Our core values shape how we operate, innovate, and deliver - every day, on every project.

On-time delivery

Commitment you can schedule.

We understand that in aerospace, oil & gas, and infrastructure, delays aren't just costly — they're critical. Our integrated planning, manufacturing, and agile logistics ensure your components arrive exactly when they're needed.

Reducing Rejections and Rework

Zero-defect mindset. Every batch

Through robust in-process controls and end-to-end quality gates, we minimize variability and ensure every part meets specification – reducing scrap, rework, and customer downtime.

Integrating product Quality,

Quality isn’t added later – it starts at design.

From Design to casting, forging to finish, quality is built into every step. We follow AS9100D, API Q1 & ISO 9001 protocols for upfront reliability, dimensional accuracy, and test-backed compliance for every fastener we ship.
