Precision-engineered superalloy component manufactured to international aerospace and defense standards. Designed for maximum performance in high-stress industrial assemblies.

Technical Specifications
Standards DIN / ISO / ASTM / NAS
Material Titanium, Inconel, Superalloys
Size Range M1.6 - M100 / Custom
Surface Passivated / Zinc / Cadmium
Standard Ref. AS9100 Rev D / ISO 9001:2015
Tensile Strength 180 ksi min (1,240 MPa)
Shear Strength 108 ksi min (745 MPa)
Max Temp. 800°F (427°C) - 1200°F (649°C)
Thread Class UNJ / MJ (Aerospace thread form)

General Description:

This high-performance fastener is specifically engineered for critical aerospace and defense applications where failure is not an option. It offers exceptional strength-to-weight ratio and superior resistance to fatigue and corrosion in harsh environments.

Key Features:

  • Manufactured from vacuum-melted superalloys for purity.
  • Rolled threads after heat treatment for maximum fatigue life.
  • Full traceability with lot control and material certifications.
  • Available with various locking mechanisms (e.g., self-locking patches, drilled heads).
